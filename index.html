Software Design and Engineering that stands out.

Charisol provides skilled Software Developers from Africa for companies seeking talents with professional and remote work experience.

Scaling software development teams.

We believe that Africa is currently the home of untapped software development talent. Our vetted Developers have what it takes to work with virtual or remote teams to build and deliver quality software products with existing projects to prove it. We choose Developers who have gained experience working with global brands like Y Combinator and understand the art of communicating in a remote working environment.

Simple. Flexible. Performant.

Each of our projects are handled with the best development practise(testing, docs, peer reviews) from initiation to a successful close. We provide support for not only our Clients, for our Developers as well through learning and growth opportunities.

We manage remote working environments with tools like Trello, Skype/Zoom, Toptal, Google Apps, Git and more.
